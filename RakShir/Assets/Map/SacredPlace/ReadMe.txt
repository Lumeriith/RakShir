================PBR Sacered Place 'Universe'===============

for built-in pipeline:
Requires Unity 5.5.0 or higher.

for HDR pipeline:
Requires Unity 2018.1 or higher.


This pack includes modular fantasy a la Sacred Place. 
- Suitable for unique game places, fantasy temples, squares. Also suitable for showcase your stuff, game lobby, VR spaces etc.;
- modular floor, ground, column;
- Textures 2K for PBR materials;
- Sacred Place desert demoscene (used precomputed GI and reflection probe);
- average tris count: ~500.

Textures:
- albedo, specular, normal, emission, height (floor).
